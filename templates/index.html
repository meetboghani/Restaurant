About Hakkasan, Bandra West, Western Suburbs.

Hakkasan is a popular restaurant of international repute. It’s known for its modern Cantonese offerings and gives its patrons an excellent meal made of high quality ingredients. Other than the a la carte menu, the restaurant’s set menu gained instant popularity with the gourmets. It allowed them to sample little bit of everything without squandering too much time, of which they are always in short of. The chef is a master of his craft. You will agree with that too after having just one bite.

Wash it all down with one of their unique refreshing cocktails. The place is highly sought after, book ahead. In desserts, mousse cake, cinnamon-pineapple ice cream, chocolate cheese cake and choco bomb will do very nicely. Finish with a pot of gourmet Chinese tea. It’s highly recommended. Similar to the menu, Hakkasan’s décor reflects the same sleek modern element. Interiors are very swish and mostly comprising of wooden finishes but the addition of electric blue LED lights in bar lifts up the entire scene.
